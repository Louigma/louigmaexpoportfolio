Digital arts are drawings or images that take less time and experience to make.

Photoshop

There are so many people that use Photoshop differently. We used it to make silly pictures like fake election posters or funny images out of a normal picture.

Some examples are: a cat with laser eyes, The Rock as the president of the USA, etc. Our projects involved discovering the AI(Artificial Intelligence) to make editing way simpler and faster. I found out that the AI has many defects but can still make things that could take several hours into a 5 minute job.
